Overall Look and Feel of Airrow
===============================

The overall look and feel should be minimalist and organized. We will avoid 3d buttons and 3d metal looks.
The Airrow website will be as simple as possible while also providing the user with enough information.
The website is targeting business audiences. It is important to remain professional and direct.
The website will have a vertical scroll and a navigation bar that directs users to different pages.

Colours
========
For Airrow, colors help simplify the Airrow software by prioritizing the clarity of the text. Professionalism is our main priority.
These are our color swatches:

- #00284d
- #ffffff
- #666699
- #ff9933
- #000000

Iconography
===========
We will focus on using icons that have are minimilistic and identifiable for any user. The icons should be simple and the same
color. 

Typography
==========
We will be testing out a number of different fonts such as Roboto, Lato, Open Sans, Montserrat from Google Fonts.

Hover
=====
Certain headlines will have an over-hover feature where once it is hovered over, it lights up to a different color. 
Colors are listed above.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be flat designed using the chosen colors outlined above.
One button will be highlighted with an up arrow to allow the user to scroll to the top of the page.
